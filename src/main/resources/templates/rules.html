The rules of the Fantasy Football Game are simple. Your fantasy team starts with a budget of 100 million. You need to sign players from real teams and assemble a squad within the budget.
Every week the players will receive a match rating based on their performance, which will contribute to your team's points. If a player from your team scores a goal, he automatically gains 5 points, which are added to the match rating.
Make your team the champion by earning the most points at the end of the season! After the season ends, everything will be reset in preparation for the next one
